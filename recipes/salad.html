Tomato Onion Cucumber Salad

Description

This salad is a perfect side to most roasted meats/kabobs. It's citrusy, salty and overall just bussin.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Lemon
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Slice Cucumber
  2. Cut tomato into wedges
  3. Slice Red onion
  4. Mix in bowl with salt, pepper, and olive oil
  5. Add lemon juice to taste
