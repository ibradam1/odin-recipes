Roasted Chicken

The fundamentals of roasted chicken: it's all about not making it dry and having some aromatics underneath. Stay tuned for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 onion
  • 3 carrots
  • Whole roasting chicken
  • White wine
  • Rosemary
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Dice onions, celery and carrots and cover bottom of roasting tray with them
  2. Season vegetables with salt and pepper and olive oil, mix
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper and rosemary
  4. Coat chicken skin with olive oil and rub
  5. Place chicken on top of vegetables and roast at 375 for 1 hour
  6. Take out when temperature reads at least 170
