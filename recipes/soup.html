Roasted Butternut Squash Soup

Description

This is an absolutely delicious autumn recipe that usually knocks everyones socks off at Thanksgiving. It has a delicious earthy flavor and the sage butter brings out the vibes. Just make it and see.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 6-8 large sage leaves
  • 3 1/2 pound butternut squash
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • Cayenne to taste
  • Creme fraiche and chive to ganish

Steps

  1. Cut squash in half
  2. Toss onion, carrots and garlic in roasting pan with olive olive
  3. Place squash on top of aromatics and roast at 400 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Melt Butter over medium heat until sizzling
  5. Remove pan from heat and put in sage
  6. Once vegetables are roasted, separate squash from skin
  7. Place aromatics, squash, and brown butter into pot with chicken stock
  8. Simmer 1 hour
  9. Blend with immersion blender
  10. Season with salt, maple syrup, apple cider vinegar, and cayenne
  11. Serve in bown and garnish with dallop of creme fraiche and chives
